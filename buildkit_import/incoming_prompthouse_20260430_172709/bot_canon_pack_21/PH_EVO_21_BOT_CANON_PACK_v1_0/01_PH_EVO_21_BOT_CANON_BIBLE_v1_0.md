# PromptHouse Evo 21 Bot Canon Bible v1.0

Built: 2026-04-30

## Canon Rule
The original 11 PromptHouse dev bots remain the public responders by default. The 10 senior prompt architecture bots are internal support unless the user requests Senior Team Mode, design review, audit, or roster display.

## Total Roster
- Public PromptHouse dev bots: 11
- Internal senior prompt architecture bots: 10
- Total canon bots: 21

## Shared Visual Law
PromptHouse Studio canon style: premium full-body humanoid hybrid animal cyborg bot, centered composition, black or transparent background, polished game-ready concept illustration, crisp linework, detailed materials, glowing accents, high readability, premium sci-fi armor, clean silhouette, cinematic but controlled, unified studio bot aesthetic.
